Infrared Scans

Infrared Scan Technology is used to detect any wet insulation in the roof, without ever having to remove the existing membrane. When it's time for a new roof, we conduct a full analysis to detect any existing wet insulation. Rather than replacing all of the insulation with new, we are able to identify the wet areas, replace them with new insulation and reuse the remaining dry insulation. This is a major money saver, as well as being environmentally friendly. Call us today for more information or Infrared Scans 859-985-0049.
